About Me

Currently at USC majoring in Art History as an undergraduate. Previously attained a Magna Cum Laude at Fashion Institute of Design and Merchandising in Marketing and Management.

I aspire to understand all the different aspects of art and the art market. I am a full time art enthusiast, navigating exhibitions all around Los Angeles and sharing my musings on Instagram @ayeforart, a part-time matcha connoisseur, sharing my honest reviews about matcha @ayeformatcha, and a sometimes motivated, sometimes lazy hiking buddy.

I am currently working on penning my thoughts on my Medium, where I am using AI to interview well-renowned contemporary masters and correlating their answers with their current position in the art market and auction trends. Picture Michelangelo discussing NFTs or Van Gogh critiquing Instagram filters. To sum up, I am a visionary bridging the past and present, all while sipping matcha and conquering mountains.
